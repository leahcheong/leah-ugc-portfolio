About me

Content that moves people

I'm Leah — a UGC creator focused on making content that actually works. Not just pretty videos, but content that makes someone pause mid-scroll and think wait, I need that.

I work across tech, beauty, and wellness, creating short-form videos for TikTok and Instagram Reels. My approach is simple: lead with the problem, deliver the payoff, leave them wanting more.

I partner with brands through platforms like Billo, Join Brands, Clip, and Insense — and I'm always open to direct collaboration too.

01

Problem-first storytelling

Every video starts with a real pain point. If it doesn't solve a problem or create a need, it doesn't make the cut.

02

Real, not curated

Polished but relatable. The kind of content that feels like a friend's rec, not an ad.

03

Platform-native execution

Built for how people actually watch — fast hooks, tight edits, captions, and formats that convert on TikTok and Reels.
